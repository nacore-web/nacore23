Advisory Board-National Conference on Research in Engineering (NACORE23)

Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kerala, India will be organizing a National level Conference on Engineering Research (NACORE23) in collaboration with ACM during May 25-27, 2023.

The primary goal of the conference is to bring engineers, academicians, scientists, industry, researchers and students to a common platform to exchange and share their experience and research results about the various aspects of Cyber Security, and to discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solution adopted,(www.nacore.in).
